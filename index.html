A note

Why can't design just be felt?

I used to arrange rooms for a living. Now I arrange interactions. The tools changed — the obsession with how things feel to inhabit never did.

I moved from the Pyrenees to Milan, then to Switzerland. Each place taught me something different about how people relate to their environments. That's still what I design: environments. Just smaller ones now.

✦

Capabilities & tools

Who?

Nerea. Born near the Pyrenees. Designing in Switzerland.

I came to interaction design through interior and exhibition design — years of thinking about how people inhabit space, move through it, feel it before they understand it. That spatial intuition is still at the core of everything I make.

Completing my Master's in Interaction Design at SUPSI, Mendrisio. Looking for internship opportunities with established studios and companies in Switzerland — and beyond.
